Kelud Mountain is an active volcano. There are many tremendous views in it. It is also a nice hiking destination. The hiking track is less dangerous. This mountain can be accessed in a regular way. We can ride our vehicle to some spot, but it will still acquire us to walk a few more to reach the top. To get to the highest top, we need to go through the hiking track. Kelud got some controversial history. It was contested by Blitar and Kediri.
